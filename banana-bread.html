Banana Bread Recipe

For the deepest flavor, use ripe bananas with lots of freckles!

About This Recipe

YIELD: 8 servings (410 Calories)
PREP TIME: 15-20 minutes
COOK TIME: 60-70 minutes
EQUIPMENT: 9x5x3" loaf pan

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Coat a 9x5x3-inch loaf pan with nonstick spray.
  3. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl.
  4. Whisk eggs, sugar, bananas, and oil in a large bowl until smooth.
  5. Add dry ingredients to banana mixture and stir just until combined.
  6. Scrape batter into prepared pan and smooth top.
  7. Bake for 60-70 minutes.
  8. Transfer to a wire rack; let bread cool in pan for 15 minutes.
  9. Run a knife around inside of pan to release the bread.
  10. Turn out onto rack and let cool completely.
